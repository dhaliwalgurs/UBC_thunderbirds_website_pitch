Annual Yule Ball

It's that time again! UBC Quidditch is hosting its third annual Yule Ball Event as a fundraiser for World Cup and regionals! This year our event will be all ages, so ickle firsties are welcome! The night will start out with some waltzing, PHOTOBOOTHING (new this year!), and socializing all under a bed of floating candles that will gradually descend into a festive rave!
